# <center> About </center> 
Welcome! I am an Economist with experience in large-scale data analysis, administrative database management, and impact evaluation of public policies. My work combines rigorous econometric methods with modern data engineering tools, including Python, R, SQL, and Databricks. I have worked with large administrative datasets from government registries and credit bureaus, built reproducible data pipelines, and applied quasi-experimental methods to estimate causal effects of public programs.
