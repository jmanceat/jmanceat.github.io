# <center> About </center> 
Welcome! I am an Economist with experience in large-scale data analysis, administrative database management, and impact evaluation of public policies. My work combines rigorous econometric methods with modern data engineering tools, including Python, R, SQL, and Databricks.

I am currently working on two projects. At Universidad EAFIT, in collaboration with Banca de las Oportunidades (Ministry of Finance) and the Inter-American Development Bank (IDB), I am designing and implementing an impact evaluation of CREO, a Colombian government program aimed at promoting financial inclusion among the popular economy. The work involves processing large administrative datasets, building reproducible data pipelines in Databricks, and estimating causal effects using quasi-experimental methods (DiD, PSM, RD).

In parallel, I am working as a Research Professional with researchers from Universidad de los Andes and Harvard University on an impact evaluation of FUNDAEC's SAT program. This project involves automated web scraping for data collection from public sources, probabilistic identity linkage across multiple administrative records, and statistical analysis in Python, R, and Stata.
